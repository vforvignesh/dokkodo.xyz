About

Welcome to dokkodo.xyz—a personal memoir exploring the intersections of money, meditations, music, and mountains.

The Name

Dokkōdō (独行道), "The Path of Aloneness" or "The Way of Walking Alone," was written by Miyamoto Musashi shortly before his death in 1645. It consists of 21 precepts on self-discipline and independence—a guide for living deliberately.

The Four Pillars

Money — Explorations in finance, markets, and the art of capital allocation.

Meditations — Thoughts, learnings, and reflections on the journey.

Music — The artists, albums, and sounds that move me.

Mountains — Adventures in the wilderness and the lessons they teach.
